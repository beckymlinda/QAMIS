## **Guidance for Mandatory Items** 

For items marked **Mandatory** (e.g., stakeholder involvement, programme learning outcomes, programme structure, and module learning outcomes), programmes should normally achieve **a score of at least 3 (Good)** to satisfy accreditation expectations, consistent with the assessment tool guidance. 

This rubric also aligns with the programme design requirements under **Minimum Standard 8.2 (Design of Academic Programmes)** in the NCHE Minimum Standards.
